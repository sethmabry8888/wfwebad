Weather advice to light the way

Simplify, improve, and protect your life.

Weather Forgetter will tell you how.

We all have our preferred source of weather information. Some of us might just look outside. For others, regularly checking an app may let us see the forecast, but...

What does a user do with all of this data?

A typical weather app can show temperature, highs and lows, precipitation, and maybe even wind speed. When it comes to making important daily decisions, should a user be required to check their app daily and interpret this data? No.

Weather Forgetter can help.

Weather Forgetter pulls live updates from your local weather service and will notify you when you want to be notified. You choose what weather events are important to you, and leave the rest to us.

Stop the worry and set your preferences.
What do you want to forget?

Weather can change your plans.
Don't let it.

You walk out to your car with just enough time to drive to work, but your windshield is covered in ice. Now you're late to the office.

Weather Forgetter won't let this happen. A reminder will come the night before saying that you need extra time. Wake up early and enjoy that cup of joe, knowing you'll have enough time to clear your windshield of ice and drive safely to work.

Let Weather Forgetter be as accomodating as you like.

Set your personal preferences so you can know about weather events most relevant to you. WF can offer help during emergency weather, or can tell you if it's a good sandal day. The mass of weather data from your local weather service will be tailored to you, to make life for you simpler, convenient, and safer.
