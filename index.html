WF v1.0


Information at your fingertips

Weather App for the Forgetful is an enhanced weather application designed to simplify, improve, and protect a user’s life. Many weather apps can provide data, such as temperature, highs and lows, wind speed, and precipitation. But what should an individual do with that data? What happens if that individual doesn’t watch the news or check their weather app to receive that information? This is what Weather App for the Forgetful was designed for.

The application will request weather data from an API based on the phone’s location. If there is a chance of ice or snow, a notification will remind the user to leave their windshield wipers up when they arrive home. This will prevent ice from forming on the wipers. Later that night, the application will send a notification to set the alarm 30 minutes earlier, to allow time for scraping ice off the windshield and for a safe and patient commute. In emergency situations, information will be provided on how to protect oneself or find the nearest emergency shelter. This information will be made common knowledge to someone who does not watch local news and will be accessible from simply clicking a notification.

Beyond these basic functions, the app may provide conveniences to the user, such as a notification if weather is expected to improve for a day. It may suggest wearing rainboots if there is heavy rain predicted. If there are high winds, it may suggest a raincoat instead of an umbrella. Our teams wants to make an app that will help you make decisions based on weather data.
